# What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (**AI**) is a branch of Computer Science that enables computers and machines to perform tasks that normally require **human intelligence**.

These tasks include:

- Learning from data
- Reasoning and problem-solving
- Understanding human language
- Recognizing images and speech
- Making decisions
- Creating text, images, music, and videos

---

## Simple Definition

> **Artificial Intelligence is the ability of a machine to imitate human intelligence by learning, reasoning, making decisions, and solving problems.**

---

## Human Intelligence vs Artificial Intelligence

| Human Intelligence | Artificial Intelligence |
|-------------------|-------------------------|
| Learns from experience | Learns from data |
| Understands emotions | Understands patterns |
| Can reason naturally | Uses algorithms |
| Makes judgments | Makes predictions |
| Can be creative | Can generate content |

## 1. Rule-Based Systems (1950s)

Machines followed predefined rules.

Example:

```
IF Temperature > 100
THEN Turn ON Fan
```

Characteristics

- No learning
- Fixed logic
- Human-written rules

---

## 2. Expert Systems (1980s)

Knowledge from domain experts was stored in a rule engine.

Example

Medical diagnosis systems

---

## 3. Machine Learning (1990s)

Instead of writing rules, machines learned patterns from data.

Example

Spam detection

---

## 4. Deep Learning (2012+)

Neural networks with many layers learned complex patterns.

Examples

- Image recognition
- Speech recognition
- Face recognition

---

## 5. Generative AI (2022+)

Creates completely new content.

Examples

- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Claude
- Midjourney

Can generate

- Text
- Images
- Music
- Code
- Videos

---

## 6. Agentic AI (Emerging)

AI systems capable of planning and performing multiple tasks with minimal human intervention.

Examples

- AI Coding Agents
- Personal AI Assistants
- Autonomous Research Agents

# Types of AI Based on Capability

---

## 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)

Designed to perform one specific task.

Examples

- ChatGPT
- Siri
- Google Translate
- Face Recognition
- Netflix Recommendations

> **Nearly all AI systems available today are Narrow AI.**

---

## 2. General AI (AGI)

Artificial General Intelligence would perform any intellectual task that a human can perform.

Status

- Research stage
- Does not yet exist

---

## 3. Super AI

AI that surpasses human intelligence in every field.

Status

- Science fiction
- Does not exist

---

# Key Takeaways

- Artificial Intelligence is the broad field of creating intelligent machines.
- Machine Learning enables computers to learn from data without explicitly programming every rule.
- Deep Learning uses neural networks with multiple hidden layers to solve complex problems.
- Generative AI creates new content such as text, images, code, audio, and videos.
- Large Language Models (LLMs) power modern conversational AI like ChatGPT.
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) enhances LLMs by retrieving relevant external information before generating responses.
- AI Agents can plan, reason, use tools, and perform multi-step tasks autonomously.
- Most AI systems in use today are Narrow AI, designed for specific tasks.
- AI is transforming healthcare, finance, education, manufacturing, retail, transportation, cybersecurity, and many other industries.
- A strong AI career starts with Python, mathematics, statistics, machine learning, and progresses through deep learning, LLMs, RAG, AI agents, and MLOps.

# Summary

Artificial Intelligence is one of the most transformative technologies of the modern era. It empowers machines to learn, reason, understand language, recognize patterns, make decisions, and generate new content. Today's AI systems, powered by Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI, are revolutionizing industries ranging from healthcare and finance to education, manufacturing, and entertainment. By mastering the fundamentals presented in this guide, learners will be well-prepared to explore advanced topics such as Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, and MLOps, paving the way for exciting careers in the rapidly evolving world of Artificial Intelligence.
